Written Histories
The more than 50 histories below give examples of faith, courage, and conversion. 

Ann Ranson Mother of Pioneer Maria Pheniex. Ann died in 1896, probably in England. History is not available. 

Aphek Woodruff  Life Sketch  Father of the Prophet Wilford Woodruff. Aphek and Beulah were also pioneers.
        Aphek Woodruff Summary Notes Wilford Woodruff's journal entry about his father.

Charles Alfred Harper Life Sketch Having not served in notable callings, he said he "felt bigger on the back seat." Yet his name is on monuments and he is honored as a great pioneer.
        Charles Alfred Harper, Wheelwright Pioneer in the original Vanguard company. Charles joined the Church in Nauvoo.
        Charles Alfred Harper, Day by Day With the Utah Pioneers PDF, 1 page. May 30, 1847 in the Vanguard company.
        Charles Alfred Harper Diary PDF, 47 pages. His journal details events of the first pioneer company to enter the valley.

Charles Alfred Harper Jr. Life Sketch  Born on the plains of Wyoming.

Dorthea Marie Thomasen Nielsen Life Sketch Suffered great losses. As an orphan, she joined the Church and became one of the early pioneers.

Eliza Spencer Moses Life Sketch Early Saint who was persecuted by family, driven from Missouri and then Nauvoo, and came to Utah as a pioneer.

Elizabeth Taylor Boyes Life Sketch  Sister of the Prophet John Taylor. Sacrificed to join the Church and became a great pioneer.

Everett Waite Jorgensen Transcribed Memories  Stories recorded by Everett close to the end of his life.

George Boyes Life Sketch  Early Nauvoo Saint who suffered losses and became a pioneer.

Hans Jorgensen Life Sketch Pioneer from Denmark who sacrificed much saying, "If it is worth anything, it is worth everything." 
        Hans and Dorthea Jorgensen Posterity Review of the stalwart family of Hans and Dorthea.
        Hans and Dorthea Jorgensen Romance Uplifting story of marriage on the plains and early life in the valley.
        Jens Hansen Life Sketch Missionary son of Hans Jorgensen who was instrumental in his conversion. 

Isaac Jorgensen Life Sketch  Craftsman who helped build the Logan Temple and was later an ordinance worker there.
        Alvaretta Leora Jorgensen Christensen Life Sketch Daughter
        Myrvin Orsman Jorgensen Life Sketch Son

James Moses Life Sketch Early Kirtland and Nauvoo Saint who protected Joseph Smith from a mob and Wilford Woodruff from a US Marshall.

Jesse Tilton Moses Jr. Autobiography  Faithful Utah Saint who served a mission to Missouri. Inspiring dream about paying tithing.
        Jesse Tilton Moses Jr. 1960 Eighty endowments in two months and other events in 1960.
        Jesse Tilton Moses Jr. Life History This life sketch shows a great amount of work accomplished for the Church.
        Jesse Tilton Moses Jr., My Birthright Jesse's view of his purpose in life.
        Jesse Tilton Moses Jr. Patriarchal Blessing Promised a long life by this blessing and Wilford Woodruff.
        Jesse Tilton Moses Jr. Tells about Wilford Woodruff  How Wilford escaped the polygamy persecutions with the protection and help of faithful Saints.
        Jesse Tilton Moses Jr. Letter to Leone Jorgensen  Response letter sharing experiences of Wilford Woodruff.
        William Earl Durrant Life Sketch  Son-in-law and husband of Mary Harper Moses. 
        Lavina Moses Jacobs Autobiography  Daughter
                Gus Twitchell Life Sketch  First husband of Lavina Moses Jacobs

Jesse Tilton Moses Sr. Life Sketch  Pioneer child and early Utah Saint who was an example of generosity.

Lars Larsen Nielsen Life Sketch  Began working for his own living at age 9. Pioneer with a story of faithful repayment to the PEF.

Lavina Harper Moses Life Sketch Faithful Utah Saint who shared her talents in many ways. She is known for her painting of Wilford Woodruff's Vermont home.
        Lavina Harper Moses - Memories of Farm Life  "In the winter evenings after the chores were done, we would play games and eat popcorn and apples and Dad would read to us out of the scriptures. Every evening was a home evening."

Lavina Wollerton Dilworth Harper Life Sketch  Faithful pioneer who drove the wagon most of the distance, until at Ft. Laramie, WY, she stopped to give birth to Charles Alfred Harper Jr.
        Lavina Wollerton Dilworth Harper Biographical Sketch Her husband would not walk down the street with her because she was a Mormon. His conversion followed.
        Lavina Wollerton Dilworth Harper Short Sketch  Nauvoo Saint, helped establish Winter Quarters, and was a talented seamstress.

Leone Moses Jorgensen Autobiography  Her life was spared miraculously on at least two occasions. Later she was a temple worker for 14 years.

Leone Moses Jorgensen Life Sketch Written in 1988 for her senior class reunion memory book.

Levi Joseph Waite Obituary Faithful early pioneer who conquered struggles and disappointment with a cheerful nature.

Lola Everett Jorgensen Life Sketch Faithful Utah Saint who loved temple work and served the youth in scouting for 35 years.
        Lola Everett Jorgensen Missionary Blessing Received this setting apart 19 months after getting married.
        Lola Everett Jorgensen Missionary Journal His travels to and from the Scandinavia mission field.
        Lola Everett Jorgensen Patriarchal Blessing "Your posterity will rejoice to know you were a God-fearing man"
        Lola Everett Jorgensen 1881-1963  Sexton for the Hyde Park Cemetery for 20 years, and other occupations.
        Norma Jorgensen Wickham Life Sketch Daughter

James Taylor Life Sketch  Father of the Prophet John Taylor. Nauvoo pioneer who walked across the plains at age 64.

John Thomas Waite Father of Pioneer Levi Joseph Waite. England Saint who died before gathering to Utah. No history available.
        Martha Ann Waite Owens Life Sketch Daughter. Faced disappointments, but in the end was able to gather with the Saints in Utah.

Maria Elizabeth Waite Jorgensen Life Sketch Faithful Utah saint. Her history details the lifestyle of early pioneers and includes moments of inspiration.
        Maria Elizabeth Waite Jorgensen Patriarchal Blessing "Your posterity will be numbered among the faithful ones in the earth. They will be God-fearing men and women and defend the principles of eternal truth, and God will pour out His spirit upon them....I bless you with a large posterity."
        Maria Elizabeth Waite Jorgensen 1884-1969 "She was baptized at the age of eight in the old Hyde Park spring."

Maria Pheniex Married Levi Joseph Waite. A pioneer who shared in his struggles, and died while giving birth. No history available.

Mary Boyes Harper Time Line Faithful Utah Saint. School teacher and Relief Society President for many years. 

Phoebe Arabell Woodruff Moses  Faithful Arizona settlement pioneer who faced challenges with Indians. Daughters of the Utah Pioneers president in Smithfield Utah.

Rebecca Nielsen Jorgensen Life Sketch  Pioneer from Denmark who had her life spared on more than one occasion.

Sarah Ann Noble Pioneer from England in her later years. (See Martha Ann Waite Owens history.)

Sarah Brown Woodruff Life Sketch Fourth wife of the Prophet Wilford Woodruff. Her life was spared in a steamboat explosion, but her father eventually died from complications.

Wilford Woodruff: A Man of Record Ensign, July 1993, 28, by Dean C. Jesse.
        Wilford Woodruff: That Your Children May Know  Ensign, Nov. 1973, 44, by Davis Bitton.
        Wilford Woodruff's Testimony Recorded on March 19, 1897. One of first recordings ever made. 
        Wilford Woodruff's Testimony, Complete Transcript  PDF, 1 page.

William Phoenix Father of Pioneer Maria Pheniex. Died in England. No history available.
