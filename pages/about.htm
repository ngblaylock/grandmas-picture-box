We've been scanning!

During the summer of 1999, Grandma allowed us to scan her collection of family histories, certificates, and old photographs into the computer with the hope of preserving what she has and in helping each of us in our family history responsibilities.  

  • Web page creation, OCR text conversion, sound file conversion, and video conversion by Ken Blaylock, 1999 - 2006.
  • Photographs provided by Leone Jorgensen and scanned by Troy Pett and Ken Blaylock, July 1999, with other photos added later. Levi H. Jorgensen's picture collection was added in 2003.
  • Written histories provided by Leone Jorgensen, Linda Blaylock, Lavina Jacobs, and the International Daughters of the Utah Pioneers, added in 2006.
  • Audio tapes provided by Linda Blaylock, and Gay Nel Barzee.
  • Video tapes provided by Linda Blaylock.
  • Other records and certificates provided by Leone Jorgensen, and Lavina Jacobs. 
  • Nauvoo records provided by the Nauvoo Lands and Records office.
