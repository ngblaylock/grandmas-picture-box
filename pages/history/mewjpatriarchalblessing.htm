Patriarchal Blessing of Maria Elizabeth Waite Jorgensen
A patriarchal blessing given by Harvey Sessions, Patriarch, upon the head of Maria Elizabeth Waite Jorgensen, daughter of Levi Joseph Waite, and Maria Phoenix Waite, born December 13, 1884, at Dayton, Idaho.

Logan, Utah, September 30, 1934.

Sister Maria Elizabeth Waite Jorgensen:

In the authority of the Holy Melchizedek Priesthood and in the name of Jesus Christ I lay my hands upon your head to give unto you a patriarchal blessing. Father in heaven draw near unto us upon this occasion for this thy handmaiden has come to me as thy servant desiring that she may have a blessing, and dictate to me the blessing Thou has in store for her.

Dear sister thou art a chosen vessel in the eyes of God our Father. You rifled your first estate in the spirit world with honor and have been permitted to come to earth in this day and age when the gospel has come to earth, and you have striven to keep the commandments of God.

You are a mother in Israel and the Lord loves you for you have been faithful in that line. You have lived in peace with your husband, and your family has learned to love you. They have been born under the new and everlasting covenant and they will be an honor to you, and your heart will rejoice as it has from childhood. Your posterity will be numbered among the faithful ones in the earth. They will be God-fearing men and women and defend the principles of eternal truth, and God will pour out His spirit upon them.

Be of good cheer for you are of Ephraim through the loins of Joseph, and every blessing God has promised to his handmaidens you are entitled to, and through your faith and integrity He will bless you. Peace will whisper to your heart and joy will fill your soul, and you will be one who all mankind will look to as one of the noble daughters of God. I bless you with every blessing which is in store for His faithful daughters. Continue your faithful labors and live near to Him; make Him your friend and you will be able to continue your work.

I seal you up against the power of the destroyer and pray God you will have strength to resist the temptations as they come to you through life, and you will know God has heard your prayers when trials and troubles come to you but you will be comforted and you will know He is your friend.

Be humble; be prayerful and faithful, and with your husband you will journey together through life. I bless you with health and strength that you will live long upon the earth and your children and children’s children for I bless you with a large posterity.

I seal you up to come forth in the morning of the first resurrection clothed with glory, immortality and eternal life, and I do it in the name of Jesus Christ and as a patriarch, Amen.

Harvey Sessions, Patriarch

Recorded by Lucy Shaw
