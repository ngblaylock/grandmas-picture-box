Postcard from Lavina Moses to Leone

The following postcard was written by Lavina Moses on 3 August 1947 to her daughter Leone. She talks about the hard work and hot whether. Leone was married at this time, evidently with three children, Rodney, Bryce, and Linda (almost 3 years old). She asks, "How are my boys and the little girl?"
